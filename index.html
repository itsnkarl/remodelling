Understanding Modern and Traditional Bathroom Styles

Modern Bathrooms

  • Clean lines and minimalistic design

  • Use of neutral colors (gray, white, black, and navy)

  • Frameless glass shower enclosures

  • Floating vanities and wall-mounted fixtures

  • Energy-efficient lighting and water-saving technology

Traditional Bathrooms

  • Rich, warm cabinetry and crown molding

  • Decorative tile work and vintage-inspired faucets

  • Freestanding tubs with soft curves

  • Classic sconce lighting and framed mirrors

  • Elegant countertop materials like marble or granite
